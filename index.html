This is for you if...

You might be ready if

  • You have been the strong one for so long you forgot what it feels like to be held
  • You are tired of performing fine when you are not fine
  • You sense there is a version of you still waiting to emerge
  • Your faith is real but something still feels unfinished inside you
  • You are done surviving and ready to actually live
  • You want a framework, not just a feeling

This is not for you if

  • You are looking for a quick fix or a 3-step formula
  • You want someone to tell you what to do without doing the inner work
  • You are not open to faith as part of the healing process
  • You are looking for crisis counseling or clinical therapy
